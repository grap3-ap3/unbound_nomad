Ohio's Triple Nickel

Published: Sun 07 May 2023
By Unbound Nomad

In Blog.

tags: Rides Routes Dealerships Ohio

Are you a motorcycle enthusiast looking for a thrilling ride through Ohio's scenic countryside? Look no further than the Triple Nickel, also known as Ohio State Route 555. This 50-mile stretch of road will test the skills of even the most experienced riders with its tight 90-degree turns and challenging terrain. But it's not just the ride itself that makes the Triple Nickel a must-see destination for motorcycle enthusiasts. Along the way, you'll encounter stunning farmland and wooded areas that offer a glimpse into Ohio's natural beauty.

In this blog post, we'll provide you with all the information you need to plan your own adventure along the Triple Nickel. From the starting point at Fink's Harley Davidson dealership to tips for a safe and enjoyable ride, we've got you covered. So strap on your helmet, rev up your engine, and let's hit the road!

Starting Point: Fink's Harley Davidson Dealership

Before embarking on your ride along the Triple Nickel, it's worth stopping by Fink's Harley Davidson dealership located on the east side of Columbus. This iconic Harley dealership has been a staple in the Ohio motorcycle community for over 40 years, offering a wide range of Harley-Davidson motorcycles, accessories, and apparel. Even if you're not in the market for a new ride, it's worth browsing their extensive selection of gear, from helmets to leather jackets.

But Fink's isn't just a place to shop - it's also a hub for the local motorcycle community. You'll often find bikers congregating outside the dealership, swapping stories and sharing tips for the best rides in the area. Fink's also hosts a variety of events throughout the year, from charity rides to bike nights with live music.

Before hitting the road, make sure to fuel up at the on-site gas station and grab a bite to eat at the adjacent restaurant, Fink's Woodland Steakhouse. With a menu featuring everything from classic steaks to seafood, it's the perfect spot to refuel before your journey.

Call to action: Whether you're in the market for a new bike or just looking to browse some of the best Harley gear around, Fink's is a must-visit destination for motorcycle enthusiasts in Ohio. So stop by and say hello before hitting the road!

The Ride

Once you've fueled up and geared up, it's time to hit the road and tackle the Triple Nickel. Ohio State Route 555 begins just a few miles south of Zanesville and runs for 50 miles through some of the most picturesque scenery in the state. But don't let the natural beauty fool you - this road is a challenge for even the most skilled riders.

The ride begins with a series of sweeping turns that wind through farmland and wooded areas, giving you a taste of what's to come. As you continue along the route, the turns become tighter and more challenging, with several 90-degree turns that require precision and skill. But it's not just the curves that make this road a test of your abilities - there are also plenty of hills and dips that require careful handling.

One of the highlights of the ride is the section known as the "Little Switzerland of Ohio," which features steep hills and stunning views of the surrounding countryside. You'll also pass by several small towns along the way, including Chesterhill and McConnelsville, where you can stop for a bite to eat or to stretch your legs.

But perhaps the best part of the ride is the chance to see Ohio's natural beauty up close. From rolling hills to lush forests, there's no shortage of stunning scenery along the Triple Nickel. Keep your eyes peeled for wildlife too - you might spot deer, wild turkeys, or even a black bear if you're lucky.

Call to action: If you're up for a challenge and ready to take on some of the most challenging and scenic roads in Ohio, the Triple Nickel is a must-ride destination. Just be sure to bring your A-game and your sense of adventure!

Tips for the Ride

While the Triple Nickel is a thrilling ride, it's important to take some precautions to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  1. Wear appropriate gear: Make sure you have the proper protective gear, including a DOT-approved helmet, gloves, boots, and a jacket. Ohio law requires all riders to wear a helmet, so don't forget this crucial piece of gear.
  2. Check your bike: Before you hit the road, give your bike a quick once-over to make sure everything is in working order. Check your tires, brakes, lights, and fluids to ensure a smooth and safe ride.
  3. Stay alert: The Triple Nickel can be a challenging road, so it's important to stay focused and alert at all times. Keep an eye out for changing road conditions, wildlife, and other hazards.
  4. Take breaks: While the ride can be completed in a few hours, it's important to take breaks to rest and stretch your legs. Stop in one of the small towns along the way for a snack or a drink, or just take a break to enjoy the scenery.
  5. Ride within your limits: It's important to ride at a pace that's comfortable and safe for you. Don't push yourself beyond your limits or take unnecessary risks.

By following these tips, you can ensure a safe and enjoyable ride along the Triple Nickel.

Call to action: If you're planning to ride the Triple Nickel, be sure to keep these tips in mind. And if you have any additional tips or suggestions, feel free to share them in the comments below!

Conclusion

Ohio State Route 555, also known as the Triple Nickel, is a hidden gem for motorcycle riders in Ohio. The challenging turns and stunning scenery make it a must-ride for any biker looking for an adventure. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a newbie, the Triple Nickel is sure to provide an unforgettable experience.

Starting your journey from Fink's Harley Davidson dealership in Columbus is a great way to kick off your ride, and the 50-mile trip to Zanesville will be filled with scenic views and plenty of twists and turns.

By following the tips we've outlined in this post, you can ensure a safe and enjoyable ride along the Triple Nickel. So what are you waiting for? Grab your bike, hit the road, and explore one of Ohio's most thrilling routes.

Call to action: Have you ridden the Triple Nickel before? Share your experience and tips in the comments below! And if you haven't ridden it yet, what are you waiting for? Plan your trip today and experience the thrill of the Triple Nickel for yourself.
