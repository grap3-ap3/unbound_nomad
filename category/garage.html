Lozen - Mistress of the Road

Meet Lozen

This machine is a MONSTER!

I bought Lozen brand new from Ocala Powersports in Ocala, Florida. When I brought her home from the dealership, in May of 2020, she had 35 miles on the odometer. And she had more than 10k miles within the first year. It's been a little slow since, with the craziness of life and a recent rebuild project, but here's the gritty details:

Engine:

  • Thunder Stroke 116 V-twin engine
  • Displacement: 1,890 cc
  • Bore x Stroke: 4.063" x 4.449"
  • Compression Ratio: 11:1
  • Fuel System: Closed loop fuel injection / 54 mm bore

Transmission:

  • 6-speed constant mesh gearbox
  • Final Drive: Belt drive, 2.2:1

Suspension:

  • Front: Telescopic fork, 46 mm diameter, 4.7 in (119 mm) travel
  • Rear: Single shock with air adjust, 4.5 in (114 mm) travel

Brakes:

  • Front: Dual 300 mm floating rotors with 4-piston calipers
  • Rear: Single 300 mm floating rotor with 2-piston caliper
  • ABS: Standard

Wheels and Tires:

  • Front: Cast 19 in x 3.5 in wheel with Dunlop American Elite 130/60B19 61H tire
  • Rear: Cast 16 in x 5 in wheel with Dunlop Elite 3 Multi-Compound 180/60R16 80H tire

Dimensions:

  • Length: 104.6 in (2,657 mm)
  • Width: 39.4 in (1,000 mm)
  • Height: 60.2 in (1,528 mm)
  • Seat Height: 26.5 in (673 mm)
  • Ground Clearance: 5.5 in (140 mm)
  • Wheelbase: 65.7 in (1,669 mm)
  • Dry Weight: 875 lb (397 kg)

Fuel Capacity:

  • 5.5 gallons (20.8 liters)

Other Features:

  • Ride Command infotainment system with 7" touchscreen display
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Keyless ignition
  • Cruise control
  • Tire pressure monitoring system
  • Heated grips
  • Power-adjustable windscreen
  • LED lighting
