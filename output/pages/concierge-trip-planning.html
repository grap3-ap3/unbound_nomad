Concierge Trip Planning

Your dream ride. My planning. Zero stress.

Why Riders Love This

Planning a motorcycle trip is fun - until it isn’t.

  • What route actually has the best curves?
  • Where are the gas gaps?
  • Which roads are construction nightmares this season?
  • Which hotels are bike-friendly (and which aren’t)?
  • How do you avoid tourist traps and ride like a local?

That’s where I come in.

You bring the idea:

“I want to ride here around this time, with this many days and this kind of vibe.”

I turn that into a rideable, realistic, dialed-in plan - so you can focus on the part that matters:

riding.

What’s Included

🧭 Custom, Rider-Specific Routing

No generic “best of” lists. No one-size-fits-all Google routes.

I build a route based on:

  • your experience level
  • your comfort with mileage and technical roads
  • your time window
  • what you actually want to see and feel

📍 Daily Ride Maps

Each day gets a clear structure:

  • start point
  • anchor stops
  • fuel windows
  • optional scenic detours
  • target end point

You always know what’s ahead - and what’s optional.

🍽️ Food & Fuel Stops

A great day on the bike can be wrecked by a bad stop plan.

I’ll help you avoid:

  • running on fumes in the middle of nowhere
  • eating whatever happens to be at the nearest exit

Instead, you get:

  • realistic fuel windows
  • vetted food suggestions
  • places that feel like part of the experience, not an afterthought

🛏️ Lodging Guidance

Where you end your day matters.

I focus on:

  • motorcycle-friendly lodging options
  • safe, visible or secure parking
  • easy morning departures
  • locations that make sense with the next day’s plan

🌦️ Safety & Weather Notes

I’ll call out:

  • elevation changes that can affect temperature
  • common weather patterns for your window
  • road types (open plains, mountain passes, tight twisties, etc.)
  • any “this can get sketchy if you’re not prepared” details

You still ride your own ride - I just help you ride it smarter.

Packages

1) The Road Map - $75

Perfect if you already have a decent idea of your trip, but want the route cleaned up by someone who actually rides.

You get:

  • a curated, refined route for your destination or loop
  • key roads and highlights
  • a simple structure that makes sense on a bike

2) The Journey Plan - $150

For riders who want more support and less guesswork.

You get everything in The Road Map, plus:

  • suggested lodging stops (cities/towns and types)
  • restaurant and food suggestions along the way
  • recommended arrival windows (to catch sunsets or avoid rush hours)
  • optional side loops or “bonus roads” if you feel good that day

3) The Full-Throttle Plan - $250

This is the “I want it all dialed in” option.

You get:

  • fully built itinerary
  • primary and backup routes
  • hazard/seasonal/weather considerations
  • day-by-day ride breakdown
  • sunrise/sunset riding windows
  • gear and packing notes tailored to your route + season

This is ideal for:

  • first big tours
  • anniversary or special-occasion trips
  • riders with limited time who need every day to count

Who This Is For

Concierge Trip Planning is a great fit if:

  • you’re planning your first big trip and don’t want to screw it up
  • you’re an experienced rider who just doesn’t have the bandwidth to plan
  • you’re riding into regions you don’t know well
  • you’re bringing a passenger or group and want to reduce unknowns
  • you want your trip to feel “effortless” once the kickstands go up

If you want a ride that feels intentional - not thrown together - this is for you.
