The Motorcycles and Music Revival is a 4-day bike rally + music festival on Loretta Lynn's Ranch. The Tennessee Motorcycles & Music Revival (commonly called TMMR or simply The Revival) is an annual Spring event that brings together motorcycle enthusiasts, music fans, and outdoor lovers for an unforgettable experience that will REVIVE your soul! The Harley-Davidson Motor Company and many of their dealers come together to welcome riders of all types and brands of motorcycles to participate and watch more two-wheeled action than any single event can offer. The musical artists range from many genres including country, outlaw country, southern rock, bluegrass and more and features some legendary acts, trending bands and many emerging artists. Outdoor lovers enjoy camping, Hurricane Creek, the trails and all of the amenities and beauty of the ranch. All across the grounds, Southern hospitality radiates..

This package includes ride time to the event, 4 days of the Revival, ride time to return your party to the local meeting point where we started. All fuel stops are planned along the way and fuel costs are included in the package, you simply pull up to the pump and fill your tank! Food options are set before the adventure begins which means you don't have to think about where to eat that next meal because it's already been decided! Your lodging and accommodation options are also laid out before the start of your adventure, and The Unbound Nomad provides your party with a complete packing list to take all the wonder and worry out of preparing for the trip!
