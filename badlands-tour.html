Badlands Tour

Posted on Sat 13 January 2024 in Rides

Distance : Ride Time
182 miles : 3.5 hours

Weave through old stone pillars and mountains and follow moderate turns and climbs through Badlands National Park. Enjoy topography that has evolved into chiseled spires, deep canyons and jagged buttes. This is an easy ride that you’ll want to take slow to fully experience the amazing scenery. Badlands National Park contains some of the most spectacular scenery of the Black Hills with deep gorges and tall spires. This ride will take you through Rapid City, Wall and Buffalo Gap National Grasslands.
